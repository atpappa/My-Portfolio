How to make a sammy

Grab your ingredients:

  • 2 Slices of sourdough bread
  • Handful of fresh spinach
  • 1/2 Avocado
  • 1 tomato, sliced thick
  • 1 cucumber, sliced thin
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  1. Heat up a skillet with a little bit of olive oil
  2. Smash up the avocado with a fork, set aside
  3. Once the skillet is hot, add your bread
  4. Take the bread out once the bottom is nice and crispy. No need to flip!
  5. With the crispy side down, add a layer of the smashed avocado
  6. Drizzle with olive oil
  7. Add tomato slices
  8. Add cucumber slices
  9. Drizzle more olive oil
  10. Sprinkle little salt and pepper
  11. Enjoy!
